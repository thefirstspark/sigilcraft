How to charge your trinity.

Sigils don't work because they're magic. They work because they create returns — your eye lands on them, your subconscious pattern-matches, your behavior shifts, reality follows. The protocol below is a 7-day initialization.

  1. Day 1 — Print. Print all three on white paper. Hold each one. Don't analyze. Just hold.
  2. Day 2 — Place. Decide where each sigil lives. Intention near where you wake. Protection at a threshold. Manifestation near where you create.
  3. Day 3 — Speak. Read each intention aloud once. Not in a special voice. In your own voice. Out loud.
  4. Day 4 — Photograph. Take a photo of each. Save as your phone wallpaper, lock screen, anywhere your eye returns to.
  5. Day 5 — Notice. Spend the day watching for what's already shifting. Don't force interpretation. Just notice.
  6. Day 6 — Touch. Touch each sigil once with your dominant hand. This locks the kinesthetic memory. Not magic. Memory.
  7. Day 7 — Forget about them. Let them work. The sigils don't need your constant attention. They need to live in your peripheral vision while your life happens.

You'll know they're working when the patterns shift. Pay attention to coincidence. That's the system showing you it heard you.
