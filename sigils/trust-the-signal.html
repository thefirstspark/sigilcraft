Trust the Signal

The truth hums beneath the noise.
◊ MEANING

Reality is loud. Feeds scroll, voices shout, algorithms pull. Underneath all of it, a quieter frequency is always broadcasting — the one that was there before you had language for it. Your gut. Your knowing. The signal.

This sigil is an antenna. It doesn't generate the signal — you do. It reminds you that the tuning dial is in your own hand, and that the static is not the truth. When you activate it, you're choosing to trust the channel that was installed in you before the world got loud.

◊ ACTIVATION RITUAL
  1. Look at the sigil. Let your eyes soften until the antenna becomes a line between you and the source.
  2. Breathe in for four counts. Hold for two. Exhale for six.
  3. Whisper or think: "The truth hums beneath the noise. I am the receiver."
  4. Close your eyes. Ask one question you've been too afraid to ask yourself.
  5. Open them. The first answer that arrives is the signal. Trust it.
